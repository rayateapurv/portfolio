About

I am Apurv Rayate, a fourth-year Design and Technology student at Parsons School of Design in New York City. In a world of complex global problems, how can people use their personal technology to interact with their local risks? I am exploring this question through the disciplines of Web and Game Development, Interaction Design and Artificial Intelligence during my time at Parsons. I thrive in a variety of technical roles and currently work as a Design Technologist at SAP.iO's Venture Studio, Unity Developer on the Machine Unlearning Project and UX Design Consultant at Cornell Tech spinout Nolana Health.

Reach out to me to chat over coffee or collaborate on an unusual project idea! I always appreciate a chance to collaborate or get feedback.
