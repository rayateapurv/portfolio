Nolana Health

Product UX for Cornell Tech Studio
Watch the snowfall as you read about the project

Nolana Health is a Public Interest start-up at Cornell Tech that makes complex discharge information accessible, relevant and actionable for patients and their caregivers.

I worked as a UX Designer for this start-up at its inception at Cornell Tech and worked on user personas, wireframes, branding and interactive prototypes. This project won the start-up award runner up funding of $30,000.
