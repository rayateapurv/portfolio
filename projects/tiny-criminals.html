Overview

Tiny Criminals is a fast paced racing game with cozy sim elements made in Godot. In Tiny Criminals, life’s all about vibing on your own terms. Plan a heist, grab some loot, then kick back on your island hideout. No stress, no rules — just sun, schemes, and a whole lot of fun. Through this project, I used GDScript to implement NPC behaviours, UI and animations. I also polished gameplay actions like the in-game shop UI functions and decoration placement UI. By working with people from multiple disciplines, I learnt to scope, test and ship a complete game product. This game was made in collaboration with Eelis Koivusaari, Michelle Lauriste, Yuchen Hu and Yu-Jung Liu.
