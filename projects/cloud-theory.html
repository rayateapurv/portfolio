Overview

I worked as a research assistant on Cloud Theory, working with Parsons game design professor Colleen Macklin. Cloud Theory is a relaxing videogame where you lay on the ground and look at clouds. Every once in a while you or your friend might see a shape in the clouds, which sparks conversation about everything from the origin of poodles to the deepest mysteries of life. For this project, I implemented a live connection between a fine-tuned GPT-Neo model hosted on HuggingFace and Unity. A demo of this game was presented by Colleen Macklin at WordHack NYC 2023. It is in ongoing development at PETLab, a game research group at Parsons.
