Description

Manic Street Preacher was a public participatory art performance held as part of Oliver Kalleinen's Interactive and Participatory Art course at Aalto University. It consisted of a Voice AI agent portraying the character of a fanatic and religious preacher hidden within a wooden box that was installed in a public space. The agent was programmed with the goal of spreading and recruiting passersby into their fictional religion.

Opportunity
Explore text generation in a non-practical, cultural context.

Role

AI Engineer

  • Built an interactive Voice AI bot using OpenAI and Gemini APIs and installed it in the lobby of Aalto's Marsio building for a day.
  • Designed and prototyped various prompts to push generative text from friendly and subservient to more emotional and manic outputs.
  • Tested various AI models (GPT-4, Gemini 2.5, Grok, Deepseek) to allow greater fine-tuning and prompt freedom. Also researched several voice AI APIs (OpenAI, ElevenLabs, Gemini, Hume AI) to find a suitable voice for the bot.

Outcomes

  • Programmed a Generative Text AI bot for live public conversation on an abstract topic like religion.
  • The project was installed and presented at Aalto University on May 30th 2025.
